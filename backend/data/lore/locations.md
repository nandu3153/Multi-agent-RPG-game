# The Moonlit Gate
**Type:** location
**Tags:** gate, moonlit, seal, threshold, ruins
**Region / Affiliation:** Northern Eldervale

## Summary
A colossal arch of black stone veined with silver light, the Moonlit Gate marks the border between civilized valleys and the Withered Expanse. It was sealed after the Ironwarden siege that claimed Bran Ironvale's brother. Runes along the keystone pulse when lunar shards pass overhead.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The gate is magically sealed and guarded by fading wards.
- Opening it requires a lunar key or immense arcane power.
- Ironwarden soldiers once camped here; their bones still litter the approach.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The seal was placed by Aldric Thorn's order, using blood from three siblings.
- Kael Thorn knows the counter-rune but will only share it for a price.

## Related Entities
- Bran Ironvale
- Ironwarden Legion
- Aldric Thorn

# The Silver Root Sanctum
**Type:** location
**Tags:** temple, healers, silver root, sanctuary, pilgrimage
**Region / Affiliation:** Order of the Silver Root

## Summary
Nestled in a grove of luminous birch, the Sanctum is the spiritual heart of the Order of the Silver Root. Pilgrims come to drink tonics brewed from moon-touched roots. The wounded are tended here, though the Order's resources are stretched thin by the refugee crisis.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Monks offer healing and shelter to the faithful.
- A wounded pilgrim near the outer shrine seeks aid.
- The inner sanctum is forbidden to outsiders.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The High Gardener hides a shard-map showing Starwell Cavern's true entrance.
- A schism brews: some monks believe the moon should remain shattered as penance.

## Related Entities
- Aldric Thorn
- Order of the Silver Root

# The Ashen Market
**Type:** location
**Tags:** market, merchants, spies, ashveil, trade
**Region / Affiliation:** Ashveil Compact

## Summary
A sprawling bazaar of canvas stalls and smoke-wreathed alleys, the Ashen Market never officially closes. The Ashveil Compact controls trade in relics, information, and contraband. Sable Dusk once ran messages through its back corridors.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Almost anything can be bought—for the right price.
- Bounty notices for guild deserters are posted openly.
- Smoke bombs and forged papers are common wares.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The Compact has a contract on Sable Dusk, payable in Starwell shards.
- A merchant named Vex Holloway reports directly to the Compact's Shadow Council.

## Related Entities
- Sable Dusk
- Ashveil Compact
- Vex Holloway

# The Starwell Cavern
**Type:** location
**Tags:** cavern, starwell, relic, underground, magic
**Region / Affiliation:** Neutral / Contested

## Summary
Deep beneath the Moonlit Gate lies the Starwell Cavern, a natural amphitheater where groundwater reflects starlight even at noon. The Starwell Relic is said to rest on a pedestal of lunar glass. Lyra Vey believes its power can restore her reputation—and perhaps the moon itself.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The cavern is treacherous: unstable floors, echoing halls, and guardians.
- Arcane energy is strongest near the central well.
- Multiple factions seek the relic within.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The relic is sentient and tests those who approach.
- Opening the Moonlit Gate is the fastest route, but triggers Ironwarden alarms.

## Related Entities
- Starwell Relic
- Lyra Vey
- Kael Thorn

# The Verdant Hollow
**Type:** location
**Tags:** forest, hollow, fey, verdant, wilderness
**Region / Affiliation:** Wild Eldervale

## Summary
An ancient forest bowl where trees grow in perfect circles and mist never lifts. Hunters avoid the Hollow after dusk. Strange lights drift between the trunks, and time feels elastic to those who wander too deep.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Wildlife here is unusually aggressive.
- Old standing stones dot the clearing at the center.
- Locals warn against accepting gifts from strangers in the mist.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- A moon-cursed dryad guards a path to a secondary Starwell entrance.
- Sable knows a safe route used by the old thieves' guild.

## Related Entities
- Sable Dusk
- Moon-Bleached Stalker

# The Ironwarden Fortress
**Type:** location
**Tags:** fortress, military, ironwarden, siege, garrison
**Region / Affiliation:** Ironwarden Legion

## Summary
A brutal citadel of iron and basalt overlooking the Greyflow River, the Ironwarden Fortress is the Legion's primary stronghold. Bran Ironvale's family shield was last seen in its trophy hall after the siege of Thornvale. The garrison is disciplined, paranoid, and hostile to mages.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Heavily fortified; direct assault is suicide.
- Deserters are crucified along the approach road.
- The Legion hunts rogue spellcasters.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Commander Vael Drust plans to seize the Starwell Relic for the Legion.
- Bran's shield is bait—rigged with a tracking rune.

## Related Entities
- Bran Ironvale
- Ironwarden Legion
- Ironvale Shield
