# The Shattered Moon of Eldervale
**Type:** world
**Tags:** eldervale, shattered moon, kingdoms, magic, calendar, mythology
**Region / Affiliation:** All of Eldervale

## Summary
Eldervale is a temperate realm of mist-shrouded valleys, ancient forests, and crumbling fortresses. Three centuries ago, the celestial body known as the Guiding Moon shattered during the War of Silver Ash, raining luminous fragments across the land. Magic became unstable; the dead do not always rest; and factions vie for relics born of lunar glass. The realm follows a twelve-moon calendar, with the Shattered Night marking the anniversary of the cataclysm.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The Guiding Moon broke into seven visible shards still orbiting Eldervale.
- Lunar fragments power artifacts and curse the unwary.
- Three major factions dominate politics: healers, merchants, and soldiers.
- Travel between regions is dangerous; roads are patrolled or abandoned.
- The common tongue is Eldervale Cant; Old Moon Script appears on ruins.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The seventh shard is invisible and anchors a prison for an entity called the Hollow Crown.
- The Starwell Relic can re-knit one shard—but at the cost of a willing soul.
- King Aldren III is a puppet; the Ironwarden Legion holds the true reins in the capital.
