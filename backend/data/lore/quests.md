# Recover the Starwell Relic
**Type:** quest
**Tags:** main quest, starwell, relic, cavern, moon
**Region / Affiliation:** Starwell Cavern

## Summary
The central quest of the campaign: locate and secure the Starwell Relic before the Ashveil Compact or Ironwarden Legion weaponizes it. The relic may heal the shattered moon—or doom whoever wields it.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The relic rests in the Starwell Cavern beneath the Moonlit Gate.
- Multiple factions compete for it.
- Lyra believes it can restore her reputation; Kael wants to sell it.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The relic chooses its bearer through a trial of memory.
- Destroying it stabilizes magic but forever prevents lunar healing.

## Related Entities
- Starwell Relic
- Lyra Vey
- Kael Thorn

# Heal the Wounded Pilgrim
**Type:** quest
**Tags:** side quest, healing, pilgrim, silver root, medicine
**Region / Affiliation:** Silver Root Sanctum

## Summary
A pilgrim collapsed on the road to the Silver Root Sanctum, feverish from a moon-fragment wound. Aldric urges aid; success improves standing with the Order and may yield a tonic or map fragment.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The pilgrim lies near the outer shrine path.
- Medicine or magic can stabilize them.
- The Order rewards those who show mercy.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The pilgrim carries a letter implicating Legion atrocities.
- Refusing aid lowers Silver Root reputation significantly.

## Related Entities
- Aldric Thorn
- Order of the Silver Root

# Recover the Ironvale Shield
**Type:** quest
**Tags:** side quest, shield, ironvale, bran, legion
**Region / Affiliation:** Ironwarden Fortress

## Summary
Bran Ironvale's ancestral shield was taken during the siege that killed his brother. Recovering it is personal—and the shield may be trapped or tracked by the Legion.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- The shield hangs in the Ironwarden Fortress trophy hall.
- Bran will support plans to retrieve it.
- Direct assault on the fortress is nearly impossible.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The shield bears a tracking rune leading enemies to the bearer.
- Returning it to Bran's ancestral hall unlocks a hidden map room.

## Related Entities
- Bran Ironvale
- Ironvale Shield
- Ironwarden Fortress
