# Order of the Silver Root
**Type:** faction
**Tags:** healers, monks, silver root, faith, sanctuary
**Region / Affiliation:** Silver Root Sanctum

## Summary
A monastic order devoted to healing the wounds of land and people. They believe the shattered moon is a spiritual injury that must be mended through compassion and ritual. Aldric Thorn is their most devoted envoy in the field.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- They offer healing and refuge to pilgrims.
- They oppose reckless use of lunar relics.
- Their symbol is a silver root wrapped around a crescent.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The Order possesses a partial map to the Starwell but will not share it with the Legion.
- Internal faction wants to destroy the Starwell Relic rather than use it.

## Related Entities
- Aldric Thorn
- Silver Root Sanctum

# The Ashveil Compact
**Type:** faction
**Tags:** merchants, spies, ashveil, trade, underworld
**Region / Affiliation:** Ashen Market

## Summary
A loose confederation of merchants, smugglers, and spies who profit from chaos. They trade in relics, secrets, and loyalty. The Compact prefers manipulation to open war, though their assassins are feared across Eldervale.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- They control the Ashen Market and most black-market relic trade.
- Information is their true currency.
- They employ couriers like Sable Dusk—until betrayal.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- They have already planted an agent in the party's orbit: Kael Thorn answers to them sometimes.
- They will pay triple for the Starwell Relic alive, not dead.

## Related Entities
- Sable Dusk
- Kael Thorn
- Vex Holloway

# The Ironwarden Legion
**Type:** faction
**Tags:** military, ironwarden, soldiers, fortress, occupation
**Region / Affiliation:** Ironwarden Fortress

## Summary
A militarist order that seized power after the moon shattered, claiming only iron discipline can hold Eldervale together. They distrust magic, crush dissent, and hoard relics as strategic assets. Bran Ironvale once served them—and despises what they became.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- They occupy the Ironwarden Fortress and patrol major roads.
- Mages are arrested on sight in their territories.
- They took Bran's family shield as a war trophy.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Commander Vael Drust seeks the Starwell to weaponize it.
- The siege that killed Bran's brother was ordered to cover a relic theft by Legion officers.

## Related Entities
- Bran Ironvale
- Ironwarden Fortress
- Ironvale Shield
