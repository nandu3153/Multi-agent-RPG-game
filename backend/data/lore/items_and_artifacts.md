# Starwell Relic
**Type:** artifact
**Tags:** relic, starwell, moon, artifact, legendary
**Region / Affiliation:** Starwell Cavern

## Summary
A palm-sized shard of pure lunar glass that pulses with starlight, the Starwell Relic is said to anchor the cavern's impossible reflections. It can mend one fragment of the shattered moon—or bind a soul to eternal vigil.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Heavy (3 inventory slots).
- Sought by all major factions.
- Radiates arcane energy near water.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Speaks in dreams to those who touch it.
- Using it requires a willing sacrifice or irreversible memory loss.

## Related Entities
- Starwell Cavern
- Lyra Vey

# Eye Sigil Amulet
**Type:** artifact
**Tags:** amulet, sigil, scrying, compact, kael
**Region / Affiliation:** Kael Thorn / Ashveil Compact

## Summary
A gold amulet bearing a winking eye, worn by Kael Thorn. It allows brief scrying and marks its wearer as Compact-affiliated to those who know the sign.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Light (1 slot).
- Kael wears it openly.
- May reveal hidden doors or listeners.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- The Compact can listen through it once per session.
- Destroying it severs Kael's Compact protection.

## Related Entities
- Kael Thorn
- Ashveil Compact

# Moonshard Blade
**Type:** item
**Tags:** weapon, longsword, lunar, magic, medium
**Region / Affiliation:** Unknown

## Summary
A longsword with a vein of lunar glass along the fuller. It hums near arcane sources and deals extra damage to moon-cursed creatures.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Medium (2 slots).
- d8+STR damage; +1d4 vs moon-cursed foes.
- Found only in deep ruins or as quest reward.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Prolonged use whispers the Hollow Crown's name.
- Can shatter the Moonlit Gate wards if struck three times.

## Related Entities
- Moon-Bleached Stalker
- Starwell Cavern
