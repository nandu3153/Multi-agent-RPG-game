# Combat Rules
**Type:** rules
**Tags:** combat, initiative, attack, damage, ac, hp
**Region / Affiliation:** Combat encounters

## Summary
Initiative: d20 + DEX modifier, highest first. Attack: d20 + STR or DEX modifier vs target AC. Damage: weapon die + relevant modifier (longsword d8+STR). At 0 HP a character is incapacitated. Combat ends when all enemies fall or all party members are incapacitated.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Critical hit on beating AC by 5+ (double damage dice).
- Ranged weapons use DEX; melee typically STR unless finesse.
- Cover and darkness may modify DC or AC at GM discretion.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Enemies use morale checks at 50% casualties unless fanatical.

# Inventory and Rest Rules
**Type:** rules
**Tags:** inventory, slots, rest, healing, encumbrance
**Region / Affiliation:** All campaigns

## Summary
Each character has 8 inventory slots. Light items = 1 slot, Medium = 2, Heavy = 3. Short rest: once between long rests, recover d6+CON modifier HP. Long rest: full HP restore and reset short rest; requires safe location.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Cannot add items if slots exceeded.
- Conditions (poisoned, frightened) persist through short rest unless cured.
- Long rest in hostile territory may trigger encounters.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Cursed items do not count toward slots but cannot be dropped without a quest resolution.
