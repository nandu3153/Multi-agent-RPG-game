# Bran Ironvale
**Type:** character
**Tags:** warrior, soldier, ironvale, shield, veteran
**Region / Affiliation:** Party / Former Ironwarden

## Summary
A broad-shouldered veteran with a scar across his jaw, Bran seeks his family's stolen shield while guarding his companions with blunt loyalty. His brother died in the Ironwarden siege of Thornvale. He distrusts magic but trusts action.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Former soldier; expert with sword and shield.
- Hunts the Ironvale Shield stolen by the Legion.
- Protective of the party; suspicious of Kael Thorn.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- He carries a hidden Legion deserter brand that could mean execution if discovered.
- He once spared Commander Drust's life—a debt Drust may collect.

## Related Entities
- Ironvale Shield
- Ironwarden Legion
- Kael Thorn

# Lyra Vey
**Type:** character
**Tags:** mage, scholar, arcane, starwell, academy
**Region / Affiliation:** Party / Disgraced Collegium

## Summary
A sharp-tongued arcane scholar expelled from the Collegium for unauthorized lunar experiments. Lyra is convinced the Starwell Relic is real and will vindicate her theories—and her name.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Expert in runes, artifacts, and lunar magic.
- Clashes frequently with Bran over magic use.
- Obsessed with reaching the Starwell Cavern.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Her expulsion was a cover: she learned the Relic's true name.
- She is tempted to use the Relic regardless of cost.

## Related Entities
- Starwell Relic
- Starwell Cavern

# Sable Dusk
**Type:** character
**Tags:** rogue, thief, courier, bounty, scout
**Region / Affiliation:** Party / Former Ashveil

## Summary
A lithe ex-courier of the thieves' guild with a bounty on her head and secrets in every pocket. Sable scouts ahead, reads danger in shadows, and deflects with sarcasm—yet she has never abandoned the party when it mattered.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Expert scout and trap-finder.
- Wanted by the Ashveil Compact.
- Knows hidden routes through the Verdant Hollow and Ashen Market.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- She stole a list of Compact agents—including Kael's name.
- She is secretly funding a safe house for guild orphans.

## Related Entities

- Ashen Market

# Aldric Thorn
**Type:** character
**Tags:** healer, monk, silver root, faith, compassion
**Region / Affiliation:** Party / Order of the Silver Root

## Summary
A calm monk of the Silver Root who sees the shattered moon as a wound upon creation. Aldric advocates mercy, tracks the party's wounds, and challenges brutal choices with scripture and steady resolve.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Healer and spiritual guide.
- Estranged from his sibling Kael.
- Helped seal the Moonlit Gate years ago.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- He contributed his blood to the Gate's seal—and Kael's as well.
- He fears the Relic will demand a Thorn sibling's sacrifice.

## Related Entities
- Kael Thorn
- Order of the Silver Root
- Moonlit Gate

# Vex Holloway
**Type:** character
**Tags:** npc, merchant, informant, market, ashveil
**Region / Affiliation:** Ashen Market / Ashveil Compact

## Summary
A silver-tongued merchant with a stall draped in ash-gray silk, Vex sells curios, rumors, and poison in equal measure. They recognize Sable on sight and may bargain for old favors—or old betrayals.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- Runs a prominent stall in the Ashen Market.
- Knows bounty prices and road conditions.
- Never gives information for free.

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Vex is the Compact's primary recruiter in the region.
- They possess a forged pass into the Ironwarden Fortress.

## Related Entities
- Sable Dusk
- Ashveil Compact
- Ashen Market
