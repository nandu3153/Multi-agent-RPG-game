# Moon-Bleached Stalker
**Type:** monster
**Tags:** beast, moon, stalker, cavern, predator
**Region / Affiliation:** Starwell Cavern / Verdant Hollow

## Summary
Pale, eyeless predators drawn to lunar light. They hunt by vibration and scent, clinging to cavern ceilings before dropping on prey.

## Known Facts (Player-Visible)
- HP: 22 | AC: 14 | Attack: +5 (claws, 1d8+3)
- Weakness: bright light (disadvantage on attacks)
- Loot: moon-bleached pelt (1 slot), occasional claw fragment

## Secrets (GM Only)
- Difficulty: Medium
- A pack leader guards the Starwell approach.

## Related Entities
- Starwell Cavern
- Moonshard Blade
